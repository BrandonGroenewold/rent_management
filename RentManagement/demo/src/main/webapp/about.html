About Us

Here at Renters United we took it upon ourselves to reimagine was it is to rent. We make it easier for someone get started renting or to find the best deals on a place to rent

Renters United focuses on helping someone just starting to rent out homes or has a spare property that they would like to make some extra money on. Using our site will provide a hassle-free way to advertise and lease out your property. You will even be able to collect rent through our website making it convenient for you and your tenant(s). With our included features you will even be able to pay the utilities or make it easy for the tenant(s) to pay utilities.

Another fantastic deal of our website is with people from the community renting out and not big corporations you can find deals you can't anywhere else. We the people set the prices and you wont pay hundreds or thousands of dollars to live in a cardboard box for two. Renters United makes it easy for you to find that place and manage your expenses that come with it as you will be able to pay your utilities and rent in one place. Feel free to search the site and contact anyone who may be renting as you can sign the lease on this website and view it anytime.
